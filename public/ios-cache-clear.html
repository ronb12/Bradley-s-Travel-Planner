Follow these steps to see the new app icon on your iPhone:

Step 1:
Remove from Home Screen
Long press the old app icon on your home screen and tap "Remove App" ‚Üí "Delete App"
Step 2:
Clear Safari Cache
Go to Settings ‚Üí Safari ‚Üí Clear History and Website Data
Step 3:
Force Refresh
Close Safari completely (swipe up and swipe away Safari)
Step 4:
Reinstall App
Open Safari, visit the app, and tap the Share button ‚Üí "Add to Home Screen"
